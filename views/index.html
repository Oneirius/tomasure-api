Tomasure API by Kugaba Inc.

Welcome to the Tomasure API, a database of meals for use with meal and calorie tracking apps!

Our database features over 3,000 meals and foods from all over the world, sourced in an organic and environmentally friendly manner!

You can access the database with the following endpoints:

"/" will take you to ...

"/user" will take you to the user details database

"/meals" is the big one, it will take you to our fully-detailed database of prepared meals and raw foodstuffs from all over the world
